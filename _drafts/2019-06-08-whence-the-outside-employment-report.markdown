Each year, I am asked to fill out an external employment form.

I don't believe I have ever had external employment to report. So, the question is, "Why do people need to report outside income?"
Secondary questions include:
* Why does my present employer care?
* why does my present employer think it has the right to know?
* To what end is this information used?
* How will they know if I have been truthful?
* What is the penalty for completing the report inaccurately?
* In which prior document did I agree to this ultimatum (accurate reporting, or else)?

Potentially humorous:
* three scenarios
** I am an exotic dancer, and make several thousand dollars a year
*** could also be "greeter at Walmart"
*** "fry cook" at a diner
*** "custodian at my church"
** I have an outside gig, doing similar work to what I am paid to do at my present employer
