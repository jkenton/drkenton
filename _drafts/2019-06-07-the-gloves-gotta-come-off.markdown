* there is too much belief in the "naked value" of Technology
("Naked value" is my term for attributing an unwarranted value to something, in the absence of supporting data or evidence; Blind faith?)

* technology can be a very valuable tool for helping learners to learn
* technology is not the source of the Learning
* technology is not responsible for changes in Performance
* to learn effectively, learners need models
* those models need to be able to provide an example to emulate
* learning does not take palce in the Technology
* at best, technology helps to mediate, or shepherd, or guide, or facilitate the conditions needed for learning to take place (in the learner's head)
